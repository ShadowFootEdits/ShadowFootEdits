Who is God?

God is the Creator of the universe, the all-powerful, all-knowing, and ever-present being who loves and cares for all creation. He is known for His attributes of love, justice, mercy, and holiness.

God as the Creator

In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth (Genesis 1:1)(check bible verses). He made everything from the vast galaxies to the smallest creatures. Humans were created in His image, giving us a unique connection with Him.

God's Love for humanity

One of the most well-known verses, John 3:16(check bible verses), tells us, "For God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life." This shows the depth of God’s love for us, offering His Son, Jesus, as a sacrifice for our sins.

The Bible as the word of God

The Bible is the Word of God, given to guide us in our lives. It contains teachings, stories, and commandments that help us understand who God is and how we should live.

Salvation through Jesus

God’s plan for salvation centers on Jesus Christ. Through His death and resurrection, Jesus made it possible for us to be forgiven and have a relationship with God. By believing in Him, we are saved from sin and can look forward to eternal life.

How to live through to god's will

Living a godly life means following God’s commandments and teachings. We are called to love God with all our hearts and to love others as ourselves. Prayer, worship, and reading the Bible are essential practices for growing in faith.
